About Bangarang Crafts

Bangarang Crafts began in 2019, but our story isn't one of a typical launch. Like many around the world, we found ourselves facing the unexpected quiet and isolation of the COVID-19 pandemic. In that stillness, something beautiful was born. What started as a way to pass the time a few stitches here, a new design there quickly grew into a passionate obsession. "Bangarang" (a word for a loud, chaotic noise) became our anthem for the vibrant, joyful creativity that cut through the silence. We discovered that even in a world that had paused, the need to create, to make something with our own hands, was more important than ever. Our crafts are more than just products; they are artifacts of resilience. Each piece is thoughtfully designed and meticulously handmade, carrying with it the spirit of finding light in the dark. We learned that connection wasn't lost it was reimagined, and we're thrilled to now connect with you through the things we make. Thank you for visiting our corner of the internet and for supporting a dream that started when the world stood still.

With years of experience and a commitment to traditional techniques, we combine artistry with practicality to deliver exceptional crafts that stand the test of time.
