About Bangarang Crafts

Bangarang Crafts began in 2019, but our story isn't one of a typical launch. Like many, we found ourselves in the unexpected quiet of the COVID-19 pandemic. In that stillness, something beautiful was born.

What started as a way to pass the time, a few stitches here, a new design there, quickly grew into a passionate obsession. Our name, "Bangarang," was inspired by the Lost Boys from the movie "Hook", and it became our anthem for the vibrant creativity that cut through the silence.

We discovered that even in a world that had paused, the need to create with our own hands was more important than ever. Each piece is thoughtfully designed and meticulously handmade, an artifact of resilience and finding light in the dark.

Thank you for visiting our corner of the internet and for supporting a dream that started when the world stood still.

Born from Resilience ‚Äì Created during the stillness of the pandemic, each piece celebrates creativity, hope, and the human need to make.
Handcrafted with Heart ‚Äì Every item is thoughtfully designed and made by hand, carrying the spirit of joy and authenticity.
Art with Meaning ‚Äì More than just crafts, each creation is a symbol of connection, light, and the beauty found in chaos.
